Bio 1/2

My name is Samira Liebendörfer I am 33 years old and am from Switzerland. I studied Visual Communication at the Academy of Art and Design in Basel with a focus on print design and typography. Afterwards I completed a few internships at an agency and smaller offices in the area of Graphic Design and Corporate Identity.
My field of expertise nowadays is UI and UX Design and I am training to become a frontend web developer.

Bio 2/2

After working as an employee and freelancer as a Graphic Designer I decided to found my own office in the field of Graphic Design together with a long-term collaborator and friend. We mainly realized work in the field of Culture and the Arts. During that time I developed an interest in frontend web development and decided to go into that direction for my career.
I am currently involved in a traineeship at Prolog, a small company in Basel specialized in Strategy, Design and Code.
With a background in Graphic Design I combine a sense for aesthetic with a preference for digital solutions, sort of a hybrid. I am interested to combine function with design to create digital experiences.
